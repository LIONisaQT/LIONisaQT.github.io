Shroommates

1 player stealth-based action PC game made using Unreal Engine. Play as a sentient mushroom trying to grow in secret!

Will you find peace and harmony with Jared the roommate? Conceal yourself from him and explore the house. Will Jared have a change of heart? Can your acts of benevolence persuade the roommate to let you live alongside him?
