Game Design and Development Projects (Pictures soon!)

Shroommates

Made with Unreal Engine

A stealth game where users play as a sentient mushroom trying to grow in secret in a UCSC student's apartment. The mushroom can grow by consuming food and staying out of direct sunlight. Players must adapt to different sizes, as size dynamically alters speed and hiding ability. Complete the objectives while trying to keep out of sight of the AI human!

MinShmup

Made with libGDX

A simple, minimalistic shoot-em up.

Beat 'Em Up!

Made with Unity

A rhythm-based fighting game. Play as fighters from various genres of music in a musical beatdown. Players are incentivized to hit on the beat to deal more damage, rather than hitting off-beat and dealing minimal damage.

World Power

A tabletop game with a satiracle look at international politics meant to represent our view of the utter insanity, childish incomprehensiblilty, and extensive cheating in the world. It is a four-player game where each player takes on the role of a country's leader and become the world's superpower through use of war, famine, disease, and politicians. Cheating is explicitly allowed and encouraged.

Snail Assalt

Made with libGDX

An Android shoot 'em up. Play as little Jimmy as he defends his home against the onslaught of snails, each with their own special abilities. Jimmy can purchase water gun and house upgrades from the shells of fallen enemies.

Food Now!

#EndIndecisiveness

Hungry and upset no one can pick a place to eat? Enter various parameters into Food Now!, such as ethnic options, travel distance, and maximum price, and get a randomly selected restaurant. End indecisiveness with Food Now!

Pick Up Boo

An Android app where users can request pickup from a contact, who will receive a text with an optional message and a Google Maps location pin of the user's location.

Gotcho Book

HACK UCSC 2017 - Creative Entrepreneurship category, 2nd place

An Android app where users can donate books on various children. The user can view various student's profiles to see their wishlist and genres they're interested in. Users can either donate a book they have or buy the book on Amazon. Books will be sent to the child's school for pickup.

Gotcho Back

HACK UCSC 2016 - Tech Cares category, 1st place

An Android safety app where the users sets a timer to get home. If the timer goes off, the user can add more time to get home. However, if the user doesn't respond, the app will ring loudly until user intervention. After a certain amount of time passes without user input, the app will text the pre-chosen contact, eventually escalating to a phone call.
